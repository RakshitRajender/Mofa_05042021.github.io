Raigarh Masterplan

Area: approx. 250 Acres
Scope of services: Concept Master Planning
Status: Design Ideation Complete
Location: Raigarh, Mumbai Poona High Way

_A pristine site having the backdrop of the Western Ghats that frames the project canvas. Natural water bodies, streams and teak plantations establish the design constraints of the project.
_An accretive approach to master planning, the driveways adhere to the natural topography of the site. The entire development is walkable and the green corridors connect the various urban spaces through shaded pathways.
_At the heart of the development are the water bodies, streams & green spaces that invite & compel the residents to explore the delightful environment.
_The plots vary from 350 to 800 Sq. yards that are categorized to accommodate Chateaus – 5000 sq. ft. villas – 3000 Sq.ft. & Dwellings 2000 sq. ft. These serve as second homes to the buyers, mostly residents from Mumbai & Pune.
